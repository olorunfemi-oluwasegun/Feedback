Welcome to the FeedMeback Hosptality app

Login

Here you can give feedback on services and amenities as well as your overall experience while staying with us

You can do this in one of two ways (or both should you choose to :) One click after the other though).

First way you can provide feedback is by leaving us a recorded message through your zoom application.

The other way is to write your feedback in with a short video attachment usually less than 5 second long.



Feedback with Zoom ?

Use this option if you have Zoom installed on your device. You will be redirected into your Zoom app from where your session will be automatically recorded. Once you end the session or leave the meeting your session will be recorded and will be sent to the appropriate team for neccesary action.





Feedback without Zoom ?

Use this option if you dont have zoom installed on your device. You would have to write in your feedback in the text area provided. After which youd be able to upload a brief video not longer than 4 seconds in cases where you need to share something visual. To upload something longer, you need to use the 'with Zoom' option.
